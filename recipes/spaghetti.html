Spaghetti

Description

Looking for some comfort food? Look no further! This spaghetti is hearty and punchy. To get a few more vegetables on your plate, try adding green bell peppers, mushrooms, spinach, and/or zucchini. Make it vegetarian by foregoing the beef; you probably won't even miss it!

Note: this recipe is copied more or less verbatim from Tastes Better from Scratch. All credit goes to the original author.

Ingredients

  • 15 oz tomato sauce
  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp dried parsley flakes
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 lb ground beef (or 1/2 lb ground Italian sausage and 1/2 lb ground beef)
  • 1 cup water
  • spaghetti noodles, for serving
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • crushed red pepper flakes, to taste

Instructions

  1. Season ground beef with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, add beef and chopped onion and brown. Drain excess grease.
  3. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, parsley, garlic powder, crushed red pepper, worcestershire, and sugar to skillet.
  4. Stir well to combine and bring to a boil. Add water and stir well.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes. Add chopped basil before serving, if desired.
  6. This sauce tastes great in Spaghetti Pie, Million Dollar Spaghetti, Slow Cooker Lasagna, and Instant Pot Spaghetti.
